About Xtra Mile

Same-day delivery, taken seriously.

Since 2000, London's most demanding businesses have trusted us with the deliveries they can't afford to lose.

Our Story

Some deliveries can be late. Ours can't.

Xtra Mile was built for jobs that don't tolerate failure — the blood sample on its way to a lab, the camera kit due on set at dawn, the part that has to land before a production line stops.

We're London's leading independent same-day courier — a dedicated fleet, GDP-trained drivers, AI-driven dispatch. Every consignment tracked. Every milestone timestamped. Nothing moves without accountability.

  • Independent. Privately owned, accountable to clients — not shareholders.
  • Dedicated fleet. Direct point-to-point. No consolidation, no compromise.
  • Real-time visibility. Live GPS, milestone alerts, full audit trail.
  • People who care. The same drivers, the same controllers, every day.
A Journey Through Same-Day

From horseback to AI dispatch.

Same-day delivery isn't a new idea — it's been reinvented every generation. Here's the road that brought us to TOM.

Chapter 01
1860's
Pony Express

Same-day urgency, on horseback. Riders staking everything on the next exchange.

Chapter 02
1960's
The Van Era

London's first dedicated same-day fleets. Rubber meets road; the modern courier is born.

Chapter 03
1980's
Radio & Paper Dockets

Controllers on radios, drivers on dockets. Speed dictated by how fast a human could shout.

Chapter 04
1990's
First PCs & Fax

Booking screens replace shouting. Faxes hum overnight. The first taste of digital despatch.

Chapter 05
2000's
Fully Computerised

Driver apps, GPS routing, electronic POD. The whole job, end to end, on a screen.

Chapter 06
2010's
Fully Automated

Algorithms allocate jobs in milliseconds. Controllers shift from gatekeepers to overseers.

Chapter 07 · Now
Today
TOM AI

Our intelligence layer. Live visibility, early anomaly alerts, and the operational picture our controllers act on.

Chapter 08 · Next
Next
Conversational TOM

A conversational interface for every client. Book, check, and update jobs in plain English — TOM understands, our team delivers.
