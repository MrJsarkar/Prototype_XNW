Modern Slavery Statement

Xtra Mile Couriers & Xtra Mile Medical is committed to preventing modern slavery and human trafficking in all areas of our business and supply chain.

We recognise our responsibility to operate ethically and with integrity, ensuring that our services are delivered in a way that respects human rights and promotes fair working practices.

Our Business

We provide same-day courier services across London and the UK, working with a network of professional drivers, partners, and suppliers.

Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Acting with honesty and transparency in all business dealings
  • Ensuring fair and lawful working conditions across our operations
  • Preventing exploitation within our workforce and supply chain
  • Complying with all relevant employment and human rights legislation

Supply Chain & Partners

We work with trusted partners and suppliers and expect them to uphold the same high standards.

We take reasonable steps to ensure that modern slavery and unethical practices are not present within our supply chain.

Due Diligence

We aim to:

  • Work with reputable suppliers and partners
  • Monitor and review our business relationships
  • Address any concerns relating to unethical practices promptly

Continuous Improvement

We are committed to reviewing and improving our practices to ensure that modern slavery risks are minimised as our business grows.

Contact

If you have any concerns relating to modern slavery or unethical practices within our business, please contact us.
